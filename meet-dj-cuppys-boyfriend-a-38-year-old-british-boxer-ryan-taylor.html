Meet DJ Cuppy's Boyfriend, 38-Year-Old British Boxer, Ryan Taylor

May 01, 2024
Post

The boyfriend of Nigerian female disc jokey, Florence Otedola, popularly known as DJ Cuppy has been revealed as Ryan Taylor.

Taylor, a British professional boxer proposed to Cuppy at the Gumball award night in Abu Dhabi where the billionaire’s daughter has been since last week.

Naija News earlier reported that Cuppy’s engagement is coming barely six months after her sister, Temi got engaged to Nigerian singer, Mr Eazi.

Cuppy, while congratulating her sister and the singer on social media, had urged the love birds to find her a man because she was also part of their love story.

However, the female disc jockey has finally said yes to her white boyfriend at a public concert, outside Nigeria.

In the video making the rounds online, DJ Cuppy, who seemed surprised when her boyfriend popped the big question, said yes and followed it with a passionate kiss on stage.

Cuppy was also seen hugging the boxer as the audience screamed and clapped in excitement.
