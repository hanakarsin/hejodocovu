Prepare For 'War' - Nigerian Pastor Warns U.S About Afghanistan, Iraq, Iran Terrorists

April 04, 2024
Post

The United States has been told to warm up for a huge attack coming from a coalition of Libya, Iran, Iraq, and Afghanistan.

According to the spiritual leader Of INRI Evangelical Spiritual Church, Primate Elijah Ayodele, the ‘war’ will be deadlier than any other.

Naija News understands that the popular clergyman predicted the scary event in a statement signed and released through his Media Office on Thursday, August 26.

Primate Ayodele said some countries had harboured a grudge against the U.S and will not forgive the exhorted nation of their many sins.

According to him, the nations forming a coalition believe that America brought terrorism and never found a solution to it till this present day.

The clergyman, however, admonished America to fortify its security, assist nations to deal with terrorism and most importantly seek God’s face to avoid the coming doom.

Ayodele’s statement reads: “Some countries have purported in their heart not to forgive the US for reasons that have to do with terrorism and in a way to retaliate, a coordinated attack is underway to set America on fire.”

“There are some countries that will not forgive America of their sins that have to do with terrorism. Some feel America brought terrorism and never found a solution to it till today. They have it in mind that America is the reason we have terrorists in almost every part of the world.

“I foresee a very serious attack on the United States of America, I don’t know when but they should expect it. It will shock them even more than the September 9/11 attack. They should start working around the clock now, and eradicate terrorism in other countries in the world if not, I see the attacks coming to them.

“I see them coming from Libya, Iran, Iraq, Afghanistan, they will form a very terrible terrorist coalition to set America on fire. This is a serious warning America should listen to, if it happens, they may not recover what will be lost in many years. Let them consult God, fortify their security system and support other countries in eradicating terrorism, this is the only way these attacks I see won’t happen,” Primate Ayodele stressed.
