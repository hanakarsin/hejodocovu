Jalang'o Tells Off Rotimi's Ex-Lover for Jealously Cautioning Vanessa Mdee: &quot;Ukiachwa Achika&quot;

  • The comedian advised his listeners to embrace the famous Kiswahili mantra "ukiwachwa wachika"

Kiss FM presenter Jalang'o has told Rotimi's ex-lover Kim Oprah to move on and give Rotimi and his new catch, Venesa Mdee, a break.

Speaking during their morning show, Jalang'o and Kamene Goro told Oprah to stop being bitter because Rotimi has already moved on.

The duo was reacting to a TikTok video in which Oprah told Vanessa Mdee not to celebrate being with Rotimi as she was once there and never had a happily ever after.

They could not bring themselves to understand why Oprah is not happy for Rotimi yet Juma Jux was happy for his ex-lover even if things did not work out.

"If you broke up with your lover accept it and move on, please. Why is it hard for you to accept?" Jalang'o wondered."It never worked with you look for a relationship that will work for you," he blasted Oprah.

Jalang’o cautions fans

In another related article published on TUKO.co.ke, the media personality offered advice to Kenyans regarding how they should handle relationships between their girlfriends and wives.

In a video shared by Kamene on Instagram, the comedian was seen passionately advising his fans to be careful of who they invest in, just in case they go broke.

Investing in friends or girlfriends was a no-no for Jalang'o as he noted they would all run away from them after becoming useless.

"Invest in your wife because when you go broke, it is her who will feed you, not the boys. If you invest in your girlfriend, if you go broke, she will leave. From how this Nairobi is set, no girlfriend wants to be with a broke man," he said.
