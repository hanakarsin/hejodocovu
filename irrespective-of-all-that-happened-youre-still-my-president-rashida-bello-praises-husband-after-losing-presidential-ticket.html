'Irrespective Of All That Happened, Youre Still My President'

April 11, 2024
Post

The wife of the Kogi State Governor Rashida Bello has showered praises on her husband Yahaya Bello after an underwhelming outing at the All Progressive Congress Presidential primary.

Naija News recalls on Wednesday, 8th June, former Lagos governor Bola Tinubu, won the APC primary after he polled 1,271 votes to defeat 13 other presidential aspirants.

Former minister of transportation, Rotimi Amaechi, came second with 316 votes, while Vice-President Yemi Osinbajo got 235 votes.

Senate President Ahmad Lawan polled 152 votes while Bello, governor of Kogi, got 47 votes.

Taking to Instagram, Rashida Bello described her husband as her superhuman stating he ran a good race to the finish.

According to Rashida Bello, irrespective of the outcome of the presidential election, her husband is still her president.

She wrote: “My Hero, My superhuman, I am so proud of you, I appreciate you more because you ran a good race to the finish. You’re still my President and always my love. Irrespective of all that happened our goal remains making Nigeria a better place and safe.”
