Regina Daniels Bags Top Appointment With 10th Assembly Senators Wives Association

February 22, 2024
Post

Nollywood actress, Regina Daniels has taken to her social media to express excitement over her appointment as an Executive Council on the Senators’ Wives Forum.

Naija News reported that the thespian was appointed as the Social Secretary of the 10th Assembly Senators’ Wives Forum Executive Council.

Speaking about her new appointment, Regina, via her Instagram page on Wednesday, disclosed that the 10th senators  wives association was hosted by her excellency, Ekaette Akpabio.

She further added that she and her colleagues were concerned about how they can help their husbands’and the First Lady of the Federation to serve the people better.

Regina stated that she looks forward to a great Nigeria.

She said: “Yesterday was the first inaugural meeting of the 10th Senator’s Wives Association of Nigeria. Hosted by our mummy Her Excellency Mrs Ekaette Akpabio, the wife of the Senate president of Nigeria.

“It was a meeting specifically about the growth of our country and ways in which we can help our senators/husbands and the First Lady of the Federation serve the great people of Nigeria better. I was also made the social secretary of the forum. I look forward to a great Nigeria.”
