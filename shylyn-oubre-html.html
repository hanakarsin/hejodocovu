Best recognized for being the wife of basketball player Kelly Oubre Jr. Her Instagram account, which operates under the handle shylynnoubre, has garnered 70,000 followers. 

Before Fame

In the year 2020, she graduated from Arizona State University with a degree in communication, business, management, marketing, and related support services.

Trivia

She is the founder and CEO of a company known as SHHY BEAUTY. In an interview with Babe Hatch, she recommended the book You Can Heal Your Life by Louis Hayes. She has visited several countries including Italy, France, and Jamaica.

Family Life

She and her husband have been married since the summer of 2022. They have a daughter they welcomed in the year 2021, as well as a son they welcomed in the year 2023. 

Associated With

In October of 2022, she posted a mini vlog of herself alongside her daughter. It was set to the song "So Sick" by Ne-Yo.
