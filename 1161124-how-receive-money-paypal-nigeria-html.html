How to use PayPal in Nigeria when withdrawing money

You cannot receive money with a PayPal Personal Account in Nigeria but can use it to send money and make online payments and buy items on e-commerce sites. Nevertheless, you can request funds from your customers by sending them invoices through your PayPal business account.

Link your PayPal Nigeria account to your debit/credit card or bank account to withdraw your PayPal funds to your Nigeria bank account. To do this, verify your PayPal account by linking your ATM card.

How do I withdraw money from PayPal to a Nigerian bank account?

There is no withdrawal limit for a verified PayPal account. Unverified accounts can only withdraw 500 per month. Follow the following steps to withdraw the money in Nigeria:

  • Log into your PayPal account via the PayPal website or app.
  • Select Withdraw Funds.
  • Click From and choose your PayPal Balance.
  • Click on To and choose your linked card.
  • Type the amount and click Continue.
  • Click on Transfer to have the money sent to your card.

Fees for transacting via PayPal in Nigeria

Sending money from your PayPal account to any part of the world is free, but receiving money has a transaction cost. PayPal will charge a conversion fee on your credit or debit card.

The recipient usually pays the fee for PayPal to convert the received funds from foreign currency to their local currency. The conversion fee changes depending on many factors, including inflation.

How to open a PayPal account in Nigeria to receive money

Use the steps below for the PayPal Nigeria sign-up process:

  • Visit the PayPal Nigeria website.
  • Tap Open a business account.
  • Enter your email address: It should not be linked with another PayPal account.
  • Enter your password: Mix special characters, numbers, capital, and small case letters to create a strong password.
  • Fill out the PayPal account opening form: Provide your legal first and last name, your legal business name, phone number, and address.
  • Select the currency you want to receive payment in.
  • Accept the terms and conditions.
  • The system will take you to the "Describe your business" page: Specify your business type and the product/service you offer and provide a website link (optional).
  • Tap Continue and provide your personal information as requested by the system: Enter your birthday, choose your nationality (Nigerian), choose a valid means of identification (Passport, national ID, Driver’s License, Permanent Voters Card), and enter your identification number as per the document you are using.
  • Provide your Home Address: Click into the Same business address check box and write the address in the text box.
  • Click Submit and confirm your email: The system will redirect you to a PayPal login page and display your email address by default. Enter your valid password (used in step 4) and click Confirm Email Address to log in.
  • Verify your identity: Ensure you upload and submit all the required documents, e.g., proof of address, proof of identity, and your photo.
  • Link and confirm your payment channel: It can be a bank account, credit, or debit card.
