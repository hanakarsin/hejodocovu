Tinubu Storms Oyo, Visits Alaafin, Olubadan-Designate (Photos)

July 29, 2024
Post

The National Leader of the All Progressives Congress (APC), Asiwaju Bola Tinubu on Sunday paid a courtesy call to the palace of the Alaafin of Oyo, Oba Lamidi Adeyemi III.

Naija News understands the visit is connected to the ongoing consultations and familiarization regarding Tinubu’s 2023 presidential ambition.

Tinubu received royal blessings from the Alaafin on his presidential ambition as the monarch prayed thus: “We have given you our real prayers because you came first and my ancestors have heard this today. The desire of your heart come 2023 will be realized….”
