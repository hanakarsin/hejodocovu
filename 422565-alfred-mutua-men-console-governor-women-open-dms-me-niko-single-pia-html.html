As some men welcomed him back to the 'streets' while showing commiseration for losing a wife, some ladies saw the perfect opportunity to shoot their shot.

Lilian announces split

Mutua's ex Lilian Nganga was the first to confirm the end of their marriage, noting she will also drop the Machakos First Lady tag.

In a statement on her Instagram page on Sunday, August 15, she maintained she and Mutua will always be friends, adding she was grateful that they were ever together.

The 36-year-old beauty queen said through her Lilian Nganga Foundation she will continue with her philanthropic works as they make her the person she is.

Mutua notes they'll remain friends

The Machakos governor disclosed that they remain on good terms, commending Lillian for being a great First Lady.

"Lillian has been an excellent First Lady and even though she will do some projects under the Lillian Nganga foundation, we have agreed she can continue with her county projects," he said.

He noted their love is just, but they needed new directions, adding she will be a great advisor as he governs his county and even in his push to become the next president of Kenya.
