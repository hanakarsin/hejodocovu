Kim Zolciak - Age, Family, Bio

Post

American reality personality who starred in the Bravo series The Real Housewives of Atlanta and her own spin-off, Don't Be Tardy. In 2015, she competed on Dancing with the Stars. 

Before Fame

She earned a nursing degree from the University of Connecticut in 1996. 

Trivia

She released a single in 2009, "Don't be Tardy for the Party," which peaked #3 on the iTunes Dance Chart.

Family Life

She married her second husband Kroy Biermann in 2011. They briefly split in 2023 before reconciling. She has six children: Brielle, Ariana, Kroy, Kaia, Kane and Kash.

Associated With

She often shared the screen with Jo Messina on The Real Housewives of Atlanta.
