1.Thor

Thor is the main character, just like he was in all the other movies that are part of the series. The Australian hunk, Christ Hemsworth play the character. In the movies, Thor is the crown prince of Asgard. A few things happen to the character in this movie, which is a little different than what happens in all the other four movies -Thor sports shorter hair than he did in the other movies. Then, there is the fact that his hammer, the Mjolnir is destroyed and he loses one eye. When asked about these plot twists, the directors said that Chris Hemsworth felt that he had portrayed the same character for a very long time, and he was ready to play around with the character.

2. Loki

Tom Hiddleston plays this character. Loki is an adoptive brother to Thor, and he is a nemesis, named after a god by the same name. Loki, as we all know him, is always mischievous and is constantly looking for new ways to be naughty. He has been the ruler of Asgard since the end of Thor-the Dark World. Loki, as we know is a trickster and is always looking for new ways to go against the rules. Loki is not necessarily interested in good governance, what bothers him, is his image.

Some of the funniest moments delivered by Korg and others in the movie

One of the most amazing things about Thor Ragnarok was the amount of humor which was in the film. Taika Waikiki, through his role as Korg, delivered most of these humorous moments. Interestingly, Taika says that when they were carving out the role, they did not want to imagine Korg would be as humorous as he ended up becoming. They intended him to say a line or two, but Christ Hemsworth liked the character so much that they even had to do motion capture for his actions. Here are some of the most brilliant hidden jokes:

  • The appearance that Matt Damon Makes: At the start of the movie, Loki is posing as Odin. The funny thing about this part is that instead of it being played by Tom Hiddleston, it is Matt Damon, in a hilariously bad wig. An even funnier turn of events is that it is not the first time that Matt has played this character, in Kevin Smith’s Dogma, he and Ben Affleck played the roles of fallen angels. The name of Matt Damon’s Angel character was none other than Loki.
  • Another Thor? Another amazing and yet very hilarious thing about this movie is that there is another character playing Thor, and it is another of the ridiculously handsome Hemsworth men, Luke Hemsworth. Luke is not as famous as Liam, which made him the perfect person to use for this gag.
  • Korg’s Misfortune: Towards the end of the film, Korg accidentally crushes his blade-armed gladiator friend. He ends up thinking that he may have killed him. While this may sound tragic, it is hilarious when you think about it from this perspective; Korg is rock and gladiator’s blades represent scissors. So it appears that rock triumphs over scissors. This joke gets even funnier. When he is trying to tell Thor about his attempt to start a revolution, but he was unable to print enough paper for the same, he is insinuating that paper beat rock.

Those are just a few of the precious moments which made this film as memorable as it was.

What Taika Waititi thinks about the character that he created?

Taika Waititi is an award-winning film director, actor, and comedian from New Zealand. He has won many awards for some of the films that he has written and directed in the past. However, a new and interesting dimension to his career opened up when he decided to play the role of Korg in Thor. He ended up creating one of the hilarious characters for the movie, and everyone is hoping that if there are sequels, they will get to see more of this rock character.

Taika states that when he was setting out to do the voice over for the character, he did not expect that it would go so well that the fans would be looking forward to more. He first captured the voice-overs for the character, and then he did the motion capture. The reason why a director was playing the role of an actor is because the rock character was supposed to be so negligible that no one would think about it. Fortunately, Taika delivered pure gold and everyone smiles when a scene brings him on.
