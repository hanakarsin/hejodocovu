Motivational Monday quotes to stay positive

March 31, 2024

The start of the workweek often triggers an overwhelming feeling of stress, sadness, and anxiety in most people. But why is Monday the most loathed day of the week? Is it because you hate your job so much or you were just too comfortable during the weekend and Monday brought about some unnecessary distraction? Well, despite your hatred for Mondays, you can actually cheer up and stay positive thanks to these motivational Monday quotes.

Many books have been written about beating Monday blues. However, you don’t need to read novels to learn how to cheer up on a Monday morning. The only thing you need to do is to understand why you are waking up in the first place and what you have to achieve by the end of the day.

  • Don’t wait on perfect conditions for success to happen; just go ahead and do something. –Dan Miller

  • Everything you want is on the other side of fear. –Jack Canfield
  • I have learned over the years that when one’s mind is made up, this diminishes fear. – Rosa Parks
  • To be successful, the first thing to do is fall in love with your work. – Sister Mary Lauret

  • Monday, here we meet again. We will never be friends, but maybe we should switch from our mutual hostility toward a more positive partnership. -Julio-Alexi Genao
