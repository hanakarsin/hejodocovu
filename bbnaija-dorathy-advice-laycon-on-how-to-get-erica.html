Dorathy Advice Laycon On How To Get Erica

August 01, 2024
Post

A housemate in the Big Brother Naija, Lockdown edition, Dorathy has advised Laycon about his feelings for fellow housemate, Erica.

Recall that Laycon declared his feelings and intention to Erica, she, however, turned him down on Tuesday after Erica noted that she is physically attracted to Kiddwaya and just mentally attracted to him.

Following the development, Laycon has distanced himself from Erica which got the attention of Dorathy.

Dorathy who noted this advised Laycon not to distance himself from Erica else he would lose her to Kiddwaya.

“If something is going to happen it’s going to happen naturally without pressure.”

“Do not tell Erica you don’t want to talk to her for a while because that way, you will push her to start hanging with Kiddwaya more.”

“That is risky because you will still be here and you’re pushing her and what if she gets comfortable with the person.”

However, Laycon in response said he does not care as long as Erica is happy.
