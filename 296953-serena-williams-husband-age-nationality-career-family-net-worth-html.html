Alexis Ohanian education and career

Alexis Ohanian attended Howard High School in Maryland, from where he graduated in 2001. He then proceeded to the University of Virginia, from where he graduated in 2005 with degrees in history and commerce.

Internet & tech entrepreneur

After graduating from university, Ohanian became an internet entrepreneur. Together with his friend, Steve Huffman, they came up with MyMobileMenu, an idea for a mobile food ordering website, which they pitched to Y Combinator, though unsuccessfully.

The company, however, had liked both them and the idea, and they encouraged them to come up with a better one, which they could fund. This led them to create reddit.com, a social networking site, which they pitched as the “front page” of the internet.

Alexis Ohanian has also dubbed in activism since he has been a vocal opponent of two bills targeting the tech industry in the years 2010 and 2011:
