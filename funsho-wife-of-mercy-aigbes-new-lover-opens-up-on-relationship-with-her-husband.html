Funsho, Wife Of Mercy Aigbe's New Lover Opens Up On Relationship With Her Husband

May 06, 2024
Post

The wife of movie marketer, Kazim Adeoti, Funsho Adeoti, has reacted to the love affair between her husband and Nigerian actress, Mercy Aigbe.

Naija News had earlier reported that the Nollywood actress confirmed her relationship with Adeoti on Sunday in a post on her Instagram account.

Mercy Aigbe on Monday also took to her account to celebrate the CEO of Ibaka TV on his birthday.

The actress who has been called out since the report emerged shared lovely pictures of herself and the movie marketer.

Some Nigerians in their reaction stated that Adeoti has the right to marry another wife as his religion permits him to marry more than one wife.

Funsho while reacting to the development in a post on her Facebook account, shared a picture of herself and her kids and thanked Nigerians for their support.

She wrote: “Losing someone who does not respect or appreciate you is actually a gain, not a loss. Thank you all.”
