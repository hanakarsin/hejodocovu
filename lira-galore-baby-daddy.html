In late 2018, Lira Galore confirmed that she was pregnant. But who was her baby daddy?

Lira Galore and her baby daddy have had a rough romantic history.

Rumors of Lira Galore’s relationship with her baby daddy, Quality Control CEO Pierre “Pee” Thomas, were confirmed in 2018 when Lira announced that she was expecting a bundle of joy on Instagram. She wrote, “I chose to keep you a secret because I had to protect the energy I was exposing you to.”

She added, “On this day Christmas 2018 I’m ready to tell everyone about you because this is THE greatest gift. Even though Daddy and I planned you and made you out of love, when I first learned that you were really coming I still was unsure and scared about bringing you into the horrors of this world.”

Lira Galore previously sued her baby daddy, Pee, for abuse.

A year after Lira and Pee’s social media spat, Lira Galore accused the Quality Control CEO of abusing her while she was pregnant and neglecting their daughter while she was in his care. While Pee had previously kept his comments off of social media, he took to Instagram to vehemently deny Lira’s claims. 

Along with accusing Lira of having a cocaine addiction, he also released a series of text messages that he claimed were from Lira.

He captioned an Instagram post, “One thing I don’t play about is my father hood [sic]. I have 16 artist [sic] on my label, I still make time to see my children four times out of the week. The only women I leave my children with are there [sic] mothers and the women I was engaged with.”

Along with being embroiled in a bitter custody battle, Pee was later fined for violating a court order that prohibited either party from discussing the case on social media. A judge later ruled that the texts posted by Pee had been altered with “false comments.” Pee was ultimately ordered to pay an $11,000 fine in addition to Lira’s lawyer fees.
