Its OK to drink alcohol any time you want Director of Public Prosecution says

February 06, 2024
Post

- It's OK to drink before hours according to a statement released by The Director of Public Prosecution (DPP)

- According to DPP, there is no such offence as drinking alcohol before hours, but selling, however, will get you arrested

The Director of Public Prosecution (DPP) has released a statement that clears up the law  governing drinking hours in the country.

According to the DPP, under the Kenyan law, there is no such offence as drinking alcohol before hours, but selling, however, will get you arrested.

“The offense that exists is selling before hours, not drinking before hours,” a letter signed by a Senior Prosecution Counsel (SPC) Job Mulati of Eldoret and confirmed by DPP states.

The letter also highlighted as an example, a man who complained to the DPP for being charged with the offense of drinking beer before hours.

Law regulating sale and consumption of alcohol are contained in the Alcoholic Drinks and Control Act of 2010 which prohibits the sale of alcohol before 5 pm on weekdays and 2 pm. on weekends and after 11 pm throughout the week.
