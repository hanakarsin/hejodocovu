Chelsea midfielder N’Golo Kante has been ranked ahead of Lionel Messi and Cristiano Ronaldo in the chase of the 2021 Ballon d’Or following his outstanding performances in recent times.

The Frenchman received the man-of-the-match award in the Champions League final having inspired the Blues to glory with their 1-0 win over Manchester City.

Kante dominated the midfield during the encounter when he recovered the ball 10 times as he also won 11 duels - with four of them aerial despite his 5ft 6height.

He was also man-of-the-match in both semi-final matches against Real Madrid and now there are calls from different quarters that the 30-year-old is the best in the world.
